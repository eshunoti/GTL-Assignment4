COVID-19 in Ghana has been a lethal catastrophe over the past few months. This paper comprehensively discusses this lethal catastrophe- COVID-19- from its foundational level to the roofing level. The rational of this paper is to help the community understand what is meant by COVID-19, and to prove to them, that COVID-19 can be curbed, even if it is described as a lethal catastrophe.

Before the resources for this paper was gained access, analytical documents which contained the whole of COVID-19 were obtained from well-known laboratories in West Africa, specifically in Ghana. Careful studies and research were also conducted to re-affirm the data in the obtained documents. From the research conducted and the data obtained, it was noticed that Ghanaians adopted a very effective and convenient methods, which has aided accurately in tracking, containing and curbing and even preventing COVID-19
